Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes KaaNuRO Group products unique?

Our formulations are designed by certified wellness associates and sourced directly from agricultural communities in Rajasthan. We combine verified scientific research with deep herbal insights, ensuring every leaf and root is selected at the peak of its biological value.

Are there any artificial preservatives in your teas or blends?

No, absolutely not. All KaaNuRO products are 100% natural, vegetarian, and free from chemical fillers, heavy metal impurities, and artificial colorants. We preserve our products naturally using vacuum-sealing protocols.

How quickly do you ship products to locations outside Rajasthan?

We deliver across India. Shipments inside Rajasthan are typically delivered in 2-3 business days. Deliveries to Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, and other metropolitan centers take 4-6 business days depending on carrier services.

Where is KaaNuRO Group located?

Our administrative headquarters and core packing units are located in Udaipurwati, Jhunjhunu district, Rajasthan, India (Pin Code: 333307). You are welcome to visit our local farms.
